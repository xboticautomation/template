High-Speed Disperser Blades for

Chemical Industry Operations.
 

The high-speed disperser or high-speed dissolver is an economical and comparatively less complicated mixing tool widely used throughout the various processes in chemical industries. It's a standard workhorse highly effective during the manufacturing of chemicals, plastics, coatings, inks, paints, adhesives, composites, and many other products. Primarily used in chemical labs, high-speed disperser comes in sizes ranging from 50mm to 6000mm. The main objective of disperser is to incorporate powders into liquid and break particles algometers to produce a fine dispersion
